    what is the game of 2048?

    The goal of the 2048 game is to combine numbers to reach the 2048 tile. Achieve the highest score possible!

    How to play On Mobile

    1. Use your fingers to swipe the screen up, down, left, or right. The tiles will move in the direction you choose.

    2. When two tiles with the same number touch, they will merge into a single tile with double the value. For example, two tiles of 2 become one tile of 4.

    3. Try to keep the board organized so you don't run out of moves. If you can't move any more tiles, the game ends.

    4. It's important to plan your moves to maximize your combinations and reach the 2048 tile!

    How to play on PC

    1. Use the arrow keys (up, down, left, right) to move the tiles in the direction you choose.

    2. Tiles with the same number will merge into a single tile with double the value. For example, two tiles of 4 become one tile of 8.

    3. Try to keep the board organized and avoid filling it with tiles. If you can't move any more tiles, the game ends.

    4. It's important to plan your moves and have a strategy to reach the 2048 tile and achieve the highest score possible!
